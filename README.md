## About Me

I build analytics that survive contact with real-world data. My MSc dissertation (CLEAN-Air Africa, University of Liverpool) meant integrating **50+ health datasets from 15+ countries** into one validated pipeline, delivering **98% classification accuracy** and improving cross-team data consistency by **25%**. Since then I've done professional data quality work on AI training datasets at a major technology client in Cambridge, and I currently evaluate AI-generated outputs as a contractor on a frontier AI data project.

What I care about: root causes over quick fixes, validation built into pipelines rather than bolted on, and dashboards that non-technical stakeholders actually use. I work daily with AI coding tools (Claude, Copilot) to ship tested analysis faster.

**Currently looking for:** Data Analyst, Analytics Engineer, and Data Quality roles in Cambridge and London.
